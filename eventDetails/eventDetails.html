Venue Islamic University of Technology Boardbazar, Gazipur
Time 04 - 07 pm

Rules and Regulations

1. Each team must consist of 3 members.
2. All participants must be students of the same university.
3. Each team must have a unique name.
4. Participants are not allowed to use any external resources during the contest.
5. The contest will consist of multiple rounds, including preliminary and final rounds.
6. The top teams from the preliminary round will advance to the final round.
7. All decisions made by the judges are final and binding.
8. Participants must adhere to the code of conduct and maintain sportsmanship throughout the event.
