About IUTCS
IUT COMPUTER SOCIETY

The Islamic University of Technology Computer Society (IUTCS) is a student-run organization committed to promoting computing excellence, innovation, and collaboration among students. Since its inception, IUTCS has organized numerous national and international level events, including programming contests, hackathons, workshops, and seminars.

Our aim is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry practices. By connecting students with professionals and providing hands-on experience, we strive to prepare future tech leaders from IUT.

Whether you're a coding enthusiast, a design thinker, or a curious learner, IUTCS welcomes you to be a part of a vibrant community driven by a shared passion for technology.

Our Vision

To foster a culture of innovation, leadership, and excellence in computing that transcends academic boundaries.

Our Mission

  • Organize national and international computing events.
  • Empower students with workshops and technical training.
  • Promote collaboration through clubs and team-based competitions.
  • Establish partnerships with industry and alumni networks.

Major Activities

  • Inter University Programming Contests (IUPC)
  • IUT Tech Fest
  • Monthly Hackathons
  • GitHub Bootcamps and Web Dev Workshops
  • Machine Learning and AI Seminars
